FAQ

Frequently asked questions about the science museum:

Q: What are the museum's hours of operation?

A: The museum is open from 10:00am to 4:00pm, tuesday, Wednesday,Thursday. Fridays we are open from 10:00am to 7:00 pm, saturdays 9:00am to 4:00pm,sunday from 9:00 to 1pm. We are closed on mondays.

Q: Is there a fee for admission?

A: no, The entrance is free for all. there is a fee of 70NOK for guided tours

Q: Is there a gift shop?

A: Yes, there is a gift shop located near the museum entrance. We offer a variety of science-related toys, books, and souvenirs.

Q: Is food allowed in the museum?

A: No, food and drinks are not allowed in the museum galleries. However, we do have a designated eating area where visitors can enjoy snacks or meals.

Q: Is the museum accessible for visitors with disabilities?

A: Yes, the museum is fully accessible for visitors with disabilities. We offer wheelchair rentals, as well as audio guides and braille labels for exhibits.

Q: Are strollers allowed in the museum?

A: Yes, strollers are permitted in the museum galleries. However, we ask that visitors be mindful of other guests and exhibit spaces.

Q: Is there parking available?

A: Yes, there is a parking lot located adjacent to the museum. Parking is free for museum visitors.

Q: Are there guided tours available?

A: Yes, we offer guided tours of the museum for groups of 6 or more. Please contact us to schedule a tour in advance.

Q: Are there any special events or exhibits coming up?

A: Yes, we have a rotating schedule of special exhibits and events throughout the year. Please check "spescial events" for the latest information.
